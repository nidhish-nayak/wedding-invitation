📸 HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
──────────────────────────
1. Take your 4 favourite photos (JPG or PNG format)
2. Rename them:  photo1.jpg  photo2.jpg  photo3.jpg  photo4.jpg
3. Drop them into the "photos" folder, replacing the placeholder files
4. Done! Open index.html in a browser to preview.

🎵 HOW TO ADD BACKGROUND MUSIC
────────────────────────────────
1. Get a South Indian instrumental MP3
   (search "Carnatic instrumental" or "Nadaswaram wedding music" on YouTube
    and download via a converter site, or use any MP3 you have)
2. Rename it exactly:  background.mp3
3. Place it in the main wedding-site/ folder (same level as index.html)

🌐 HOW TO PUBLISH (Netlify — Free, 2 minutes)
───────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Make sure you've added your photos and music first
2. Go to:  https://app.netlify.com/drop
3. Drag and drop the entire "wedding-site" folder onto that page
4. You'll get a live link like:  https://random-words.netlify.app
5. Click "Site settings" → "Change site name"
   → Set it to something like:  prashanth-ashwini-2026
   → Your link becomes:  https://prashanth-ashwini-2026.netlify.app
6. Share that link on WhatsApp! 🎉
